Smart tools, smarter cities

April 2016

Proof of concept User analytics Product validation Hypotesis testing

This was a part Lean Startup, part Agile Software effort that I've made during my time at SGS Economics and Planning.

The challenge

In any consulting practice, project findings are typically condensed into lengthy reports that offer little opportunities for client to interact and better understand the statistics behind them. The business wanted to improve this situation by offering analytical products that allowed clients to have real time access to data and statistics.

Solutions

Hypothesis testing

I began integrating interactive visualisations into newsletters and blogs, using them as ‘experimental’ releases that would be used by our clients. I tested different features and improved the technology stack iteratively by asking for feedback from colleagues and monitoring analytics of the site for each release.

Analytics

I measured the product’s viability by recording the the time required to build and release a new visualisation that would satisfy the optimum amount of users.
