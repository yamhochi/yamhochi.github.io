About me

Four years ago, I ventured beyond the design world in search of career and personal growth. By chance, I stumbled upon an unusual combination of user experience design, urban planning and consulting.

About My work

Since then I've worked with visually impaired people to improve their wayfinding abilities using assistive devices. I’ve worked with urban planners and economists to model best locations for infrastructure investments. I combined my knowledge in geographic information systems, design and web development, and helped a government agency develop a platform to raise awareness about the environment.

what i enjoy the most

Seeing my designs come to life, turning an abstract idea into something tangible, scratching a usability itch, knowing that I've made someone's life better because of that. These were just a few of the many things I truly enjoyed througout this journey. Perhaps the greatest satisfaction of all cames from the ability to influence decisions and helping others
