Finding Hotspots

Sometimes one size fits all

I find myself mapping the same maps a lot. Each client may have a different set of criteria, but ultimately the process of creating the map is quite mechanical and reproducible. I've been finding ways to give my clients a solution that allows them to assign their own criteria to identify "hotspots". Suitability mapping is one of those maps that needs this solution.

What is a Suitability Map?

This Suitability Map enables strategic planners, investors and citizens identify most suitable locations for different housing and employment lands uses.

Users can assign weights to a series of spatial criteria to generate a heat map to show locations the desired criteria. For example, Rail weighs 10 while Retail may weigh only 5 because people tend to rely on trains a lot more than they rely on shopping centres.

Bootstrap, CartoCSS and Cartodb.js and Torque.js for these visualizations are available in this gist.
