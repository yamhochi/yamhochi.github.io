About Me

4 years ago I deviated from my normal path. That turned out to be the best thing I've ever done.

I moved out of my comfort zone, did things that I normally wouldn't.

I became better at communicating. People didn't understand my work at first because I spoke a different language. So, I learned to speak their language. I learned to speak from small details to really high level. When people noticed my words, they also noticed my work.

In design, communication is as important internally, as it is externally. As a designer, I care about letting the business know what I do to help them achieve their business goals, as much as I care about the users.

I my seem a bit out of the box. None of my previous jobs had similar descriptions, but that's just me, I take risks and I make the best out of it.
