About A.YAM

I am a data visualization specialist with backgrounds in spatial science and urban planning. To me, data visualization and data science enables me to communicate ideas, influence decisions, and make an impact in social, environmental and civic outcomes. In my current position, I can regularly be found visualizing and reimagining cities using spatial theory and data models. I hack , speak and write about all things maps and I can be found rambling on Twitter and Github. I grew up in a small town in Borneo, received my Masters from the University of New South Wales, and has lived in Sydney, Australia since 2012.
