About me

Four years ago, I ventured beyond the design world in search of career and personal growth. By chance, I stumbled upon an unusual combination of user experience design, urban planning and consulting (view my cv).

About My work

Since then I've worked with visually impaired people to improve their wayfinding abilities using mobile devices. I’ve worked with urban planners and economists to model and visualise best locations for infrastructure investments. I combined my knowledge in geographic information systems, design and web development, and helped a government agency develop a platform raise awareness about the environment.

what i enjoy the most

Seeing my designs come to life, turning an abstract idea into something tangible, scratching a usability itch, knowing that I've made someone's life better because of that, these are just few of the many things I enjoy as a designer. Perhaps the greatest satisfaction of all, is the ability to influence decisions and help others
